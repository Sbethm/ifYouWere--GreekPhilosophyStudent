Humans have been grappling with the origins of our existence and the rules that govern our world for over thousands of years. Many cultural groups and different time periods have tried to find reason, logic, and meaning. The Ancient Greece was one of the most influential times for western philosophy and their ideas have shaped our world today. But, what was it like for the students of this time? Who were they?
